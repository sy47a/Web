Korea According to the title and Article 1 of the Constitution of the Republic of Korea, the official national name is the Republic of Korea, and according to the State Council Notice No. 7 "On National Names and Some Local Names and Map Colors" enacted on January 16, 1950, the abbreviations are Korea and Korea.

The official English national code is Republic of Korea. South Korea and Korea are used as English abbreviations and general names, and KR and KOR are used as national codes.
